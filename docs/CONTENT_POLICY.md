# Content Policy

This repository is an open user-research skill and learning library. It is not a dump for copyrighted material, private customer data, or internal-only content.

## Allowed Content

- Original prompts and workflow instructions
- Original templates, examples, and checklists
- Original summaries of research methods
- Transformed notes from books or articles
- Small attributed excerpts when legally safe and necessary
- Public-domain or permissively licensed material with attribution

## Disallowed Content

- Full books, PDFs, or chapter reproductions
- Long copyrighted excerpts copied into markdown
- Raw customer transcripts or research data that contain personal or confidential information
- Private internal docs that are not approved for public release
- Secrets, credentials, or live tokens

## Book Content Rule

Book-related content must be transformed into original working material for agents and researchers. That means:

- summarize the framework in your own words
- extract reusable checklists or heuristics
- convert methods into prompts or templates
- record where the idea came from

Do not upload page scans, full chapter notes, or long verbatim passages.

## Research Data Rule

Examples in this repo must be synthetic, anonymized, or fully public.

If an example starts from real research:

- remove names, companies, and identifying details
- remove secrets and private URLs
- rewrite exact quotes unless you have explicit permission to publish them

## Attribution Rule

If a file is inspired by or adapted from an external source:

- add the source to `docs/THIRD_PARTY_ATTRIBUTIONS.md`
- note the source and license if reuse goes beyond generic inspiration
- prefer paraphrase and transformation over direct copying

## Promotion Rule

Content should be agent-usable. Prefer material that helps an agent:

- scope a study
- write a better brief
- draft an interview guide
- synthesize evidence
- produce a stakeholder-ready readout

Avoid adding decorative or motivational content that does not improve execution quality.
