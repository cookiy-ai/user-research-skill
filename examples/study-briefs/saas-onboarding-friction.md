# Example Study Brief: SaaS Onboarding Friction

## Raw Business Question

"Why are so many trial users dropping before they finish setup, and what should we change first?"

## Stronger Research Brief

### Objective

Understand where new workspace admins lose momentum during onboarding, what they expect to happen next, and which friction points are actually blocking activation.

### Key Learning Questions

- How do new admins describe the job they are trying to complete on day one?
- Where do they hesitate, abandon, or seek outside help?
- Which setup steps feel mandatory versus confusing but optional?
- What alternatives or workarounds do they use when setup stalls?

### Recommended Method

- 6 exploratory interviews with recent trial users who started but did not complete setup
- Optional product-walkthrough prompts if screenshots are available

### Target Participants

- Small to mid-sized SaaS workspace admins
- Signed up in the last 30 days
- Started onboarding but did not reach activation

### Sample Size

- 6 participants
- Enough to identify the main activation blockers without over-scoping the first study

### Cookiy-Ready Description

We want to understand why recent SaaS workspace admins drop out during onboarding before activation. Focus on their day-one goal, what they expected the setup flow to do, where they got stuck, what felt risky or confusing, and what would have helped them continue.
