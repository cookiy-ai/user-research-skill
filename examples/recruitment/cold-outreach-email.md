# Example: Cold Outreach Emails (Synthetic)

These are **fabricated** templates. Edit for your brand, locale, and legal requirements.

## A — Short LinkedIn-style message

Subject: Quick research interview on [topic] (30 min, [incentive])

Hi [Name],

I am researching how [role] teams handle [workflow problem]. I am looking for people with experience in [specific criterion].

If you are open to a 30-minute remote interview, I can share [incentive]. No sales pitch—just questions about your workflow.

If interested, reply with a good time this week or book here: [scheduling link].

Thanks,
[Researcher]
[Organization]

## B — Slightly warmer referral tone

Subject: [Mutual connection] suggested I reach out

Hi [Name],

[Mutual connection] mentioned you have deep experience with [context]. I am running a short research study on [topic] and would value your perspective.

The session is 30 minutes, remote, and compensated with [incentive]. Your answers are confidential and used only for product discovery.

Would [day options] work?

Best,
[Researcher]

## C — Follow-up for non-responders

Subject: Re: Quick research interview (30 min)

Hi [Name],

Following up once in case my note got buried. Still interested in a 30-minute interview about [topic]?

If not a fit, a quick "no thanks" helps too.

Thanks,
[Researcher]
