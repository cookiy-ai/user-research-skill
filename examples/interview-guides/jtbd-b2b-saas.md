# Example: JTBD Switch Guide (B2B SaaS, Synthetic)

This is a **synthetic** example for style and structure. It is not a real customer transcript.

## Context

- Product category: team collaboration software
- Audience: IT-adjacent team leads at mid-size companies
- Goal: understand switching from spreadsheet workflows to a dedicated tool

## Interview guide (abbreviated)

### Warm-up

- What is your role and what are you accountable for week to week?
- How does your team coordinate work today across tools?

### Struggling moment

- Tell me about the last time a spreadsheet-based workflow caused a real problem for your team.
  - What was the trigger?
  - Who felt the pain first?

### Search and evaluation

- When did you start looking for something better?
- What did you try before buying anything?

### Switch

- What made you choose the approach you use now?
- What almost made you stay with the old way?

### Proof and onboarding

- What had to be true in the first two weeks for you to feel confident?

### Close

- If you were advising someone in your position, what would you tell them to watch for?

## Moderator notes

- Push for a specific incident with dates and roles.
- Separate "tool features" from "team process constraints."
