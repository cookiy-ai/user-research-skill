## 1. Warm-up (3-5 min)

- Build rapport; explain roles
- Explain there are no wrong answers; looking for honesty
- Confirm incentives and confidentiality boundaries

## 2. Context (5-8 min)

- Role and responsibilities
- Relevant workflows and tools
- Recent changes that matter to the topic

## 3. Core topic modules (repeat as needed)

For each module:

- Module goal: [ ]

  - [ ] open exploration questions
  - [ ] task or scenario (if applicable)
  - [ ] probes for specificity
- Evidence to capture: [ ]

## 4. Concept or prototype reaction (optional)

- First impression without coaching
- Task-based walkthrough
- Debrief: what matched expectations vs surprised them

## 5. Wrap-up (3-5 min)

- Anything we missed?
- Who else experiences this problem?
- Thank-you and next steps

## Moderation guardrails

- Ask for stories and examples before opinions
- Avoid leading the participant to confirm your hypothesis
- Prefer silence over helping too quickly during tasks
