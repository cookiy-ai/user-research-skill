# Findings Deck Template

A stakeholder-friendly outline for research readouts. Adjust depth for the audience.

## Slide 1 — Title

- Study name
- Date range
- Team and contributors

## Slide 2 — What we wanted to learn

- Decisions this research supports
- Non-goals and scope boundaries

## Slide 3 — Who we talked to

- Participant criteria
- Sample size and limitations
- Recruitment caveats

## Slide 4 — Methods

- Interviews, usability tasks, surveys, analytics (as applicable)
- What we could and could not observe

## Slide 5 — Key takeaway (one sentence)

- The headline conclusion

## Slides 6-9 — Findings (one theme per slide)

For each theme:

- Theme title
- What we learned (claim)
- Evidence (short quote or metric)
- Implication (so what)
- Confidence (high/medium/low) + why

## Slide 10 — Recommendations

- 3-5 actionable recommendations
- Expected impact and effort (if known)

## Slide 11 — Open questions and next steps

- What we still do not know
- Proposed follow-up research

## Slide 12 — Appendix pointer

- Full quotes, detailed tables, study materials location

## Presentation rules

- One idea per slide
- Prefer participant language over researcher jargon
- Separate observation from interpretation from recommendation
