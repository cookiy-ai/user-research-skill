# Continuous Discovery Loop — Method Card

## What it is

A lightweight operating model for teams that want steady learning from customers while shipping. The emphasis is on **weekly touchpoints**, clear outcomes, and rapid experiments rather than one-off research projects.

## Core loop (conceptual)

1. **Outcome**: define the customer and business outcome you are pursuing.
2. **Opportunities**: identify problem spaces grounded in evidence.
3. **Solutions**: generate and test small experiments.
4. **Learning**: update beliefs; prune weak bets early.

## Rituals that make it work

- Standing interview cadence with diverse customers
- A visible map from outcomes → opportunities → solutions (see OST prompt)
- Decision rules for when evidence is strong enough to scale

## Outputs

- A prioritized opportunity backlog
- Experiment logs with learnings
- Reduced reliance on big-batch research

## Common pitfalls

- Confusing activity (many interviews) with progress (clear decisions)
- Letting the roadmap hide assumptions
- Skipping synthesis, which causes repeated questions
